Key Observations

  • Large θ_E lenses (>2"): Arcs often visible even in ground-based
  • Small θ_E lenses (<1.5"): Arcs often blurred into galaxy, hard to see
  • Blue arcs: Easier to see against red elliptical
  • SLACS/BELLS: Confirmed with HST, but arcs may NOT be visible in DR10
